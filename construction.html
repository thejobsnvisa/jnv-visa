This boom creates exceptional opportunities for skilled tradespeople, project managers, and construction professionals at all levels.

From high-rise buildings in Sydney and Melbourne to mining infrastructure in Western Australia and Queensland, construction professionals are in high demand.   • White Card (Construction Induction)
  • Relevant trade licenses and certifications
  • Working at Heights certification
  • Safety equipment and PPE knowledge

Skills & Experience

  • Trade qualifications or relevant experience
  • Physical fitness and manual dexterity
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Teamwork and communication skills

Documentation

  • Valid driver's license
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Right to work in Australia
  • Professional references
